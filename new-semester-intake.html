New Semester Intake 2026

Date: April 1, 2026

The seminary warmly welcomes a new cohort of entrants for the upcoming academic year. These young men have answered God’s call and will begin their journey of formation in faith, philosophy and pastoral ministry.

Orientation programs will introduce them to the rhythms of seminary life, including prayer, study, community service and spiritual direction. We invite family, friends and benefactors to pray for our new seminarians as they begin this important chapter.

Details about the orientation schedule, faculty introductions and course offerings will be shared in the coming weeks. Please stay tuned through our Seminary TV channel and bulletin for updates.
