Ordination Anniversary of Rt. Rev. Constantine Rupiny

Date: May 12, 2026

On this special day, we commemorate the ordination anniversary of our beloved former rector, Rt. Rev. Constantine Rupiny, now serving the Church as a bishop. His years of dedicated service to the seminary and the wider diocesan community have inspired countless seminarians and laity alike.

The anniversary celebration will include a thanksgiving Mass, reflections on Bishop Rupiny’s pastoral journey, and messages from students and alumni whose lives he has touched. We encourage all members of the seminary community to join us in offering prayers of gratitude and petitions for continued strength and grace in his episcopal ministry.

Join the livestream of the anniversary Mass on our Live page and share your own stories and blessings for Bishop Rupiny in the comments section.
