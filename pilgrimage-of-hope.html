Pilgrimage of Hope

Date: June 8, 2026

Our seminary community embarks on a sacred journey to the Holy Door as pilgrims of hope. This pilgrimage is an opportunity for reflection, penance and renewal, echoing the footsteps of the Uganda Martyrs whose courage and faith continue to inspire our mission.

Participants will gather at the seminary for prayer and send-off before travelling to the pilgrimage site. Throughout the journey, we will observe moments of silence, communal prayer and spiritual talks to deepen our understanding of mercy and redemption.

Upon arrival, pilgrims will enter through the Holy Door, celebrate the Eucharist and receive special blessings. We invite all seminarians, alumni, families and friends to unite with us in spirit by watching the pilgrimage coverage on our Live stream and sharing in the grace of this year of hope.
